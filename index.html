VINCI

The mission of Vinci is to provide high-quality and well-made, timeless handbags while providing customers with confidence and elegance.

Luxury, Elegance, Timeless

Vinci is an Italian designer that handmakes authentic 100% Italian leather purses. We are a designer brand that is luxurious, elegant, and timeless. The organization makes handbags from small to large and comes in a variety of different colors. The company started in 2016 and is headquartered in New York.
